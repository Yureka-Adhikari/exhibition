A little about ICT

ICT stands for Information and Communications Technology, which is a broad term that describes a variety of tools and resources used to create, store, and share information. ICT is an extension of information technology (IT), but it emphasizes the integration of computers and telecommunications, as well as the importance of unified communications.


About Online Games

An online game is a video game that is played over the internet or other computer network, and can be played on a variety of devices:

Devices

Online games can be played on personal computers, laptops, mobile phones, game consoles, and PDAs.

Types

Online games can be categorized as skill-and-action games, strategic games, or online RPGs.

Examples

Some examples of online games include World of Warcraft, Everquest 2, The Sims Online, and Second Life.
